Popular destinations across Sri Lanka

These are some of the most loved places travelers choose when building a Sri Lanka itinerary. They can be combined into private tours, couple trips, family holidays, or group adventures.

City

Colombo

The energetic capital city, perfect for a first taste of Sri Lanka with shopping, dining, culture, and modern city life.

Urban Food Shopping
Culture

Kandy

A cultural heart of Sri Lanka, known for its sacred temple, scenic lake, and strong connection to the island’s heritage.

Temple of the Tooth Lake Views Heritage
Hill Country

Nuwara Eliya

Cool weather, tea estates, and colonial charm make this one of Sri Lanka’s most refreshing mountain escapes.

Tea Country Cool Climate Scenic
Scenic

Ella

One of the island’s most popular mountain towns, loved for viewpoints, train rides, waterfalls, and relaxed vibes.

Nine Arches Bridge Hiking Views
Iconic

Sigiriya

Famous for the ancient rock fortress, Sigiriya is one of Sri Lanka’s most iconic destinations for history and unforgettable views.

Ancient Site Photography Culture
Wildlife

Yala

One of the best destinations in Sri Lanka for wildlife lovers, known for safaris, leopards, elephants, and raw natural beauty.

Safari Leopards Nature
Beach

Mirissa

A beautiful southern beach destination known for whale watching, palm-lined shores, and laid-back coastal energy.

Beach Days Whale Watching Relaxed
Coastal Heritage

Galle

Historic charm meets ocean views in Galle, where travelers can enjoy the famous fort, cafes, boutique stays, and coastal walks.

Fort History Ocean Views
Adventure

Arugam Bay

A favorite on the east coast for surfing, beach life, and travelers looking for a more free-spirited coastal experience.

Surfing East Coast Beach Town
Ancient City

Anuradhapura

Step into Sri Lanka’s deep history through sacred sites, ancient ruins, and an atmosphere filled with cultural significance.

Heritage Sacred Sites History
Beach

Trincomalee

Known for calm beaches, clear waters, and a peaceful coastal atmosphere, Trincomalee is ideal for sea lovers and relaxation.

Nilaveli Ocean Relaxation
Leisure

Bentota

A great destination for beach stays, lagoon experiences, and easy-going coastal holidays with comfort and variety.

Lagoon Tours Beach Hotels Water Activities
